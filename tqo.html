Game overview

An adventure-dialog-race game on the theme of order and disorder.

Made for the WonderJam UQAC Winter 2018.
Winner of the public's choice reward!
Unity, C#, custom animation system
7 devs
Made in 48h, Winter 2018
I worked on: all aspects

Embody the mage Cafarnum in this twin-stick shooter, face the Great Order that invades your country and freezes all their inhabitants for its sole purpose: order (no disorder). Use your powers with the glyph system, collect resources from the locals by dialog and destroy the monsters on your way to reach the heart of the mountains and face your nemesis.

Design details

The genres

The following descriptions have been given by the jam staff. This section does not give details on the game himself, but we used these definitions to design the game.

Continue Reading / Close

Adventure

An adventure game is a game where the player directly controls the movement and actions of a hero. The latter progresses in a scenario clearly expressed through the game. By progressing in the game and facing various challenges, the protagonist acquires new skills, abilities and progresses in history.

Dialog

A dialogue game is a game in which the player must interact with the inhabitants of the world and then make decisions according to the proposals and reactions of the different characters and vice versa. The player's reaction to the dialogue will have a direct influence on the game's environment and these are essential to achieving certain game goals.

Race

A racing game is a game whose goal is usually to progress as fast as possible from one point to another point to win. The search for speed is usually about getting to the goal first, beating a countdown, reducing a completion time or just to survive.
