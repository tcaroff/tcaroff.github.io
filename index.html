About me

I've been passionate about video games since I was a kid, when my father made me play WarCraft seated on his knees while I couldn’t even talk yet. Subsequently, I discovered a passion for science, technology, computer ... and all that mixes well!

Stuff I Love:
1) The tech: real-time destruction, physics simulations, procedural generation and many cool other things. I try new tech things in every project I make.
2) Experience: find how to achieve a design intent, how to achieve the desired player experience.
I think gaming is above all an experience. And yes, tech can create new experiences.

You also can meet me at some Meetups groups in Montréal, mostly at C++ Montréal.
