Game overview

An arcade-race team-versus game with innovative coop gameplay.

Unity, C#, FMOD
4 devs, Scrum
4 weeks
I worked on: code (multiplayer handling, optimisation, copilot gameplay, ...), game gesign, UX & 2D Art

Car Crash BOOM !!! Yeah! is a racing game in cooperation. The teams are consisting of two players: a pilot who is responsible for driving the vehicle, and a co-pilot who has special abilities to attack other vehicles, defend his team, and assist his driver. During the cooperation phases controls and goals change to put the teams to the test.

The pitch

This is the first day of sales, make sure you arrive first in front of your favorite store!
As a result, several small old cars with cannon and gunner on the roof run at full speed on unlikely circuits. All the means are good to arrive first, so that the drivers and their co-drivers do not hesitate to tackle and trap, try to send their opponents into the ditch, throw objects to each others, or even take dangerous shortcuts. All of that being obviously damaging to the local population, wildlife, flora, and public finances. Cooperation is the only thing that will allow our teams to reach their goals. Defend your car, fight the others, and get there first, whatever the price.
